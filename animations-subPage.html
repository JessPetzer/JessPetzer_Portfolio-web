ANIMATION

Growing up, I’d lose hours in animated shows — drawn to their ability to transport, inspire, and tell stories in ways nothing else could. That early love for imaginative worlds shaped how I see design today. Animation became a way of thinking out loud — of turning abstract, non-linear ideas into something dynamic and immersive. Whether it's creating motion graphics, marketing visuals, or character-driven narratives, the driving force behind it all is chasing that feeling — creating moments that resonate and feel shared, not just shown. With a solid grounding in storyboarding, a unique illustrative style, and high proficiency in Adobe After Effects, I strive to bring concepts to life in ways static visuals simply can’t.
