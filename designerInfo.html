Let me introduce myself...

01

STORY

Hi there, I'm a 24-year-old digital designer from Durban, with a dream of branching out into front-end web development and diving into the world of anime as I focus on 2D character animation. My love for animation started when I was a child. I wasn't very social and had high anxiety levels as a person 'on the spectrum', so film and television became my escape. They were my window to the world and taught me how to communicate. I always hoped to create something that could offer others the same comfort and inspiration that I found in those stories. If my work can make even a small difference in someone else's life, I'll feel like I've served a purpose.

02

EXPERTISE

Up until a year ago I had studied BA in Digital Design at Vega School of Design, where I developed a strong foundation in visual storytelling and a broad skill set ranging from motion graphics to 3D modeling. However, more recently I have spent the past several months applying these skills in the iGaming industry, working within the production space to design marketing assets and integrate game-ready content into development pipelines. Now known for my unique illustrative style and strong foundation in storyboarding, I bring concepts to life through expressive movement and thoughtful design— creating engaging digital experiences across platforms with a detail-oriented approach, a strong work ethic, and a genuine passion for tackling new creative challenges that help me grow and evolve as a designer.

03

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

My design philosophy revolves around creativity, craftsmanship, functionality, and aesthetics. I embrace diversity and innovation, always striving to create designs that leave a lasting impression. For me, design is about more than just looking good — it's about connecting with people on a deeper level and giving my work a sense of purpose.

04

UNIQUE SELLING POINTS

What sets me apart is my commitment to meticulous detail and a strong work ethic. I aim to create designs that are not only beautiful but also meaningful and impactful. At its core, my work is driven by a desire to make a difference - no matter how small - in someone else's life through creative expression.
