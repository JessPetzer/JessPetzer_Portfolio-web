MORE ABOUT ME

私について

STORY

Heyo, I'm a 23-year-old digital design student from Durban, with a dream of branching out into front-end web development and diving into the anime industry in Japan, focusing on 2D character animation. My love for animation started when I was a child. I wasn't very social and had high anxiety, so film and television became my escape. They were my window to the world and taught me how to communicate. I always hoped to create something that could offer others the same comfort and inspiration that I found in those stories. If my work can make even a small difference in someone else's life, I'll feel like I've fulfilled my purpose.

DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

My design philosophy revolves around creativity, craftsmanship, functionality, and aesthetics. I embrace diversity and innovation, always striving to create designs that leave a lasting impression. For me, design is about more than just looking good—it's about connecting with people on a deeper level and giving my work a sense of purpose.

EXPERTISE

I have recently studied a 3-year BA in Digital Design at Vega School of Design, where I have developed a wide range of technical skills and interests. I'm detailed-oriented, thorough, and have a strong work ethic. I'm always on the lookout for new and exciting projects that challenge me and allow me to grow. My expertise spans various design disciplines, from traditional to digital mediums.

UNIQUE SELLING POINTS

What sets me apart is my commitment to meticulous detail and a strong work ethic. I aim to create designs that are not only beautiful but also meaningful and impactful. My work is driven by a desire to make a difference, no matter how small, in someone else's life through my creative expressions.
