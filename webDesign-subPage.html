WEB DESIGN

With roots in web development, I’ve built a strong foundation in HTML and CSS throughout my studies, while steadily growing my skills in JavaScript & Python. I feel I thrive in the space where technical problem-solving meets creative design — the thrill of finally solving a complex bug after endless patience and persistence and the satisfaction when everything finally 'clicks' (pun intended). In today’s digital world where first impressions matter most, I see web design as more than clean code or good layout & visuals, but as a powerful way to shape how audiences connect and resonate with a brand. Being able to influence that moment through thoughtful design and functionality is something I consider a powerful tool in my creative arsenal. I don’t just design websites — I create lasting impressions.
