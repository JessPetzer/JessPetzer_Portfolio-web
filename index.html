Well, hello there!

My name is Jess, and I am an aspiring animator and front-end web developer, representing the Oubaitori brand.

The ancient Japanese idiom "Oubaitori" comes from the kanji for four spring-blooming trees: cherry, plum, peach, and apricot. Each tree blooms at its own pace, symbolizing that everyone grows and develops individually. Oubaitori encourages personal growth, celebrating individuality, and avoiding comparisons with others. It emphasizes that, like these trees, each person has a unique journey and distinct contributions.

As an artist, I believe each work, no matter how different, has its own unique value. There is no single way to be an artist; I embrace new mediums and believe in simplicity and minimalism to highlight the true beauty of the work.
